Our Services

Luxury Car Service offers full-facility services beyond general repair. We offer the best service in the auto industry in Las Vegas at a reasonable price. We perform a range of different services including:

  • Smog Checks
  • Oil Change
  • Tune-ups, distributor caps/rotors, ignition wire sets, timing belts
  • Brake Repair & Anti Lock Braking System (ABS) Repair

  • Transmission Fluid
